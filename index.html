Symphonyx

Symphonyx is an immersive audio-based puzzle game set in a mysterious forest, where players rely on their senses of hearing and touching to navigate through a visually obscured environment. As the screen remains black, players are thrust into a world where sound becomes their guide and haptic feedback becomes their connection to the game world. To progress through the game, you must keenly listen to the soundscape and analyze the subtle audio cues. Riddles and puzzles are scattered throughout the forest, and they can only be solved by interpreting the sounds and vibrations around you. Each riddle poses a unique auditory challenge, requiring you to decipher patterns, recognize harmonies, and detect subtle variations in the environment.

Unori

Unori is an educational app designed specifically for iPad, offering an interactive way to learn American Sign Language (ASL). One of the standout features of Unori is its camera integration, powered by advanced machine learning models. Users can practice their signing skills by performing the signs in front of the iPad's camera. Unori's machine learning model analyzes the user's signing and provides instant feedback, offering helpful suggestions to improve accuracy and fluency. The app's user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation make it easy to explore and learn at one's own pace.
